The Grand Unifying Theory of Food Categorization

When it comes to food, there are three categorizations:

  • A sandwich is any food which is attempting to be contained, but still has at least one open face.
  • A calzone is any food which is attempting to be contained, but has no open faces.
  • Finally, a salad is any food which does not attempt to be contained.
